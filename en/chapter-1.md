## 1. The Eternal Motion and Interconnectedness of Matter

### 📜 Engels' Original Text (1875)
> "The whole of nature was shown to be moving in eternal flow and cyclical course... All rigidity was dissolved, all fixation dissipated, all particularity that had been regarded as eternal became transient."

### 🔬 21st-Century Scientific Commentaries

#### 🔹 Support: Chaos Theory & Non-linear Science
Engels intuitively realized that nature is a whole composed of "countless interwoven movements," but the Newtonian mechanics (determinism) of his time could not provide mathematical support.

**Chaos Theory**, developed in the late 20th century, perfectly supports this dialectic:
* **The Butterfly Effect** proves the non-existence of isolated systems; any tiny local change is amplified into global fluctuations through non-linear feedback.
* Matter is not a stagnant, perfectly predictable machine, but evolves in self-organization "far from equilibrium." This reshapes Engels' assertion of "eternal flow" with contemporary mathematics.
